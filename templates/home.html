Where Engineering

Takes Shape.

A curated collection of mechanical models, assemblies, and technical drawings.

3D Model

Engineering concepts brought into three dimensions.

The collection includes mechanical components modeled from standard engineering drawing references and textbooks.
Each part was independently designed in CATIA V6 to develop proficiency in solid modeling, geometric design, and CAD workflows.

Assemblies

Individual components assembled into working mechanical systems.

Explore complete mechanical assemblies including Screw Jack, Knuckle Joint, Pipe Vice, Plummer Block, Press Tool, Tool Head of Shaper, and Motor Blower. Each assembly includes all constituent parts along with the final assembled model.

Drawings

Technical documentation derived from 3D models.

A collection of engineering drawings generated digitally from CAD models, featuring dimensioning, annotations, and standard drafting practices used in mechanical design and manufacturing.
